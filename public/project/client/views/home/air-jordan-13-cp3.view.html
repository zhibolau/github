Air Jordan 13 CP3

This Chris Paul version of the Air Jordan 13 celebrates his 10th anniversary in the NBA. The shoe is dressed in a Black, Sunstone, and Orion Blue color scheme to match CP3’s old New Orleans Hornets colors – the team that originally drafted him. Featuring a Black leather and mesh with 3M reflective detailing, along with Blue suede overlays. Other details include Yellow hits on the ‘CP’ branded tongue, as well as hitting the outsole.
